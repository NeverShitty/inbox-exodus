Three-Phase Workflow

Our AI-driven analysis never blindly moves your files. You stay in control of the entire process.

Phase 1: Analyze

Extract and classify files from Microsoft 365 using AI analysis.

  • Connect to Microsoft 365
  • Extract files and emails
  • Analyze content with GPT
  • Flag litigation-relevant terms

Phase 2: Propose

Generate folder structure proposal based on AI classification.

  • Group by entity, type, purpose
  • Create optimized structure
  • Identify duplicates
  • Present for your approval

Phase 3: Execute

Migrate files to Google Workspace after your approval.

  • Connect to Google Workspace
  • Create approved structure
  • Migrate files with integrity checks
  • Generate comprehensive audit logs
